## Short tagline

Control your Sonos speakers — including AirPlay streams — straight from Stream
Deck. Live volume, play/pause, and next-track, with auto-discovery.

## Description

**Volume for AirPlay Sonos** turns three Stream Deck keys into a live remote for
any Sonos speaker on your network — whether it's playing a streaming service,
line-in, or an AirPlay stream from your Mac, iPhone, or iPad.

### What's included

- **Volume Up** and **Volume Down** keys that don't just send a command — each
  key continuously redraws itself with the speaker's **real current volume**: a
  large percentage readout, direction chevrons, and a fill bar along one edge.
  Stack Volume Down directly under Volume Up and the two bars line up into a
  single continuous 0–100% gauge.
- A **Play / Pause** key that shows what a press will do — a play glyph when the
  speaker is idle, a pause glyph while it's playing — and **skips to the next
  track on a double-press**.

### How it works

- **Auto-discovery.** Open any key's settings and pick your speaker from a
  dropdown that lists the Sonos devices found on your local network. No Sonos
  account or app required. Prefer to type it? Manual IP entry is available as a
  fallback.
- **Always in sync.** Every key reads the speaker's volume, mute, and playback
  state on a short poll loop, so the display stays correct even when you change
  things from the Sonos app, the AirPlay source, or another key.
- **Local only.** Everything runs over the local Sonos device API — no cloud
  service, no login, no third-party servers.

### Configurable

- Volume step: 1%, 2%, 5%, or 10% per press
- Volume bar on the left or right edge of the key
- Show or hide the percentage on each key (hide it on one of a pair to avoid
  showing the number twice)

### Requirements

- Stream Deck software 6.9 or later
- macOS 12+ or Windows 10+
- A Sonos speaker on the same local network
